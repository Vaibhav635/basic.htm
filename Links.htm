Bill Gates

William Henry Gates III (born October 28, 1955) is an American businessman and philanthropist best known for co-founding the software company Microsoft, with his childhood friend Paul_Allen, He later held the positions of chairman, chief executive officer (CEO), president, and Systems_architect, of the company. Gates was also its largest individual Shareholder, until May 2014. He was a pioneer of the microcomputer revolution, fof the 1970s and 1980s
