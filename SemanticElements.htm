WAFCO (Wellness And Fitness Care Options)

Features of the WAFCO Spinal Brace Belt:

  • Solid Metal Bars: Equipped with four aluminum bars, the belt offers strong and reliable support to the lower back, helping to reduce pain and fatigue.

  • Elastic Strap for Custom Fit: The elastic strap allows for adjustability on both ends, ensuring the right amount of compression to relieve back pain and promote comfort.

  • High-Grade Cotton Fabric: The belt is made from durable, soft cotton fabric, providing comfort and breathability during extended use.

  • Versatile Use: Ideal for post-surgery recovery (e.g., slipped disc surgeries), osteoporosis treatments, or everyday use to improve posture, especially during long work hours.

  • Lumbar Spine Compression: Designed to apply the right amount of compression to the lumbar area, aiding in pain relief and support.

  • Long-Lasting Durability: Constructed with high-quality materials to ensure long-term effectiveness and wear resistance.
